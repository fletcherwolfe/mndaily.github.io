P

rospect Park is home to single-family homes and a few apartment complexes. The Witch's Hat Tower dominates the forested Tower Hill Park in the center of the neighborhood. The Stadium Village commercial district is part of the area, and has recently seen massive growth in student housing. The neighborhood's winding roads are lined with Queen Anne-style houses from the early 20th century. The state recently designated Prospect Park as a historic neighborhood.
