S

outheast Como's single-family homes and small shops contrast sharply with its abandonend industrial sites and railroad tracks. The neighborhood sits northeast of the University of Minnesota's East Bank and is home to student renters and long-term homeowners alike. A General Mills research lab there recently came under fire after it was revealed the company had dumped toxic chemicals in the neighborhood.
